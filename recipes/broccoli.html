Lemon Garlic Roasted Broccoli with Parmesan

Description

Lemon Garlic Roasted Broccoli with Parmesan is a simple yet flavorful dish made with broccoli florets roasted in the oven with olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
  2. Toss the broccoli florets with olive oil, garlic, salt, and pepper in a large bowl
  3. Spread the broccoli florets in a single layer on a baking sheet
  4. Roast for 20-25 minutes, or until the broccoli is tender and slightly browned
  5. Remove from the oven and toss with Parmesan cheese and lemon juice
  6. Serve immediately
