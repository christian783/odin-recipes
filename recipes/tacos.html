Tacos

Description

Tacos are handheld, versatile Mexican dishes consisting of various fillings wrapped in tortillas.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Filling like ground beef or chicken
  • Toppings like chopped onions,cilantro or tomatoes
  • Extras like hot sauce or chopped radishes

Steps

  1. Choose your tortillas
  2. Cook your filling
  3. Chop your toppings
  4. Warm your toppings
  5. Fill your tortillas with toppings
  6. spread your preferred toppings on them
  7. squeeze and bite
